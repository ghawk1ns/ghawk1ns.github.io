SURE Walk is a student-run volunteer group that provides walks to and from campus to UT students, faculty, and staff, with the help of student volunteers. Volunteers are gathered from trusted organizations from around campus, such as Orange Jackets and APO to name a few, and include both males and females.

Location: SAC 2.106
Hours of Operation Monday – Thursday from 10 p.m. – 2 a.m.
Phone 512-232-9255
Email surewalk.utsg@gmail.com
Agency Director: Leigh Larson

An agency of Student Government, SURE Walk is in service Monday through Thursday from 10 p.m. – 2 a.m. during the fall and spring semesters. Upon ordering a walk, two volunteers, one male and one female, will meet you at your current location and walk you to your destination, whether it be on campus or in the greater campus neighborhoods.
